 Diarrhea

  Ingridents:

  • Beleric Dried skin
  • Black Salt
  • Alum Stone
  • Water

  Procedure:

  1. Add 10 grams Beleric Dried skin,10 grams Black Salt,5 grams Alum Stone smash it and make it as powder.
  2. Pour 100 ml of water in a basin and boil it 5 mins. Make sure Don't change the quantity of the Ingridents.
  3. Now take that Mashed powder and put it in a paper and make it as Tea bag.
  4. Keep the powered bag above the boiling water in such a way that the water vapour pases through the powered bag.
  5. Then take the powdered bag and keep it in sun until it dries.
  6. For adults add 5 grams of powder with Butter milk and For childrens add 2 or 3 grams of powder with honey and water .

 Acid reflux

  Ingridents:

  • Honey
  • Water
  • Turmeric powder
  • Jeera
  • Grated ginger
  • Cardamom

  Procedure:

  1. Cut the small piece of ginger and add it in water and boil it.
  2. Take the extract and add some turmeric powder and honey.
  3. Mix well and drink.
  4. Add two pieces of cardamom in water and drink instead of plain water.
