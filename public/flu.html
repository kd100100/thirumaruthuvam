Cold & Flu

 Cough

  Ingridents:

  • Tailed Pepper
  • Honey
  • Alpinia Officinarum
  • False Daicy White leaf
  • Ginger

  Procedure:

  1. Add Sliced Ginger pieces and 9 pieces of Tailed pepper and smash it and keep it aside.
  2. Grind False Daicy White leaf and keep it.
  3. Now add half a teaspoon of Alpinia Officinarum powder,Honey and False Daicy White leaf paste.
  4. Now add slicedd ginger and tailed pepper paste mix well and eat.

 Sinusitis

  Ingridents:

  • 10gm Leucas flower
  • 10gm Ponni Rice
  • 1 Dried Chilli
  • 100ml Gingerly oil

  Procedure:

  1. Boil the Gingerly oil until medium heat and add ponni rice,Dried chilli,Leucas flower and Mix it.
  2. The oil extract is ready. Filter the extract and keep it.
  3. Weekly once or twice using this oil Take a hair bath.
  4. By continuous usage of this oil the problem will get cured.

 Common Cold

  Ingridents:

  • 15gm Cinnamon Bark
  • 10gm Thippili
  • Honey
  • 10gm Black pepper

  Procedure:

  1. Add Cinnamon Bark,Thippili,Black pepper smash it until it becomes powder.
  2. Take 5gm of smashed powder and mix it with Honey and eat it.
  3. This medicine can be taken twice a day.

 Fever

  Ingridents:

  • 10gm Nilavembu
  • 10gm korai kilangu
  • 10gm Dried ginger
  • 10gm Sathakuppai
  • Water

  Procedure:

  1. Add 100ml of water in a basin and boil for 5 min.
  2. Now add Nilavembu,korai kilangu,Dried ginger and Sathakuppai and boil it.
  3. Now filter the extract and drink it twice a day.
