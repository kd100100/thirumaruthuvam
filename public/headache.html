Headache

 Headache

  Ingridents:

  • Beetroot
  • Cloves
  • Betel Leaves

  Procedure:

  1. First, grind a few cloves into a fine powder and also extract the juice of beetroot by grinding and filtering it.
  2. Now to 1/2 tablespoon of clove powder, add 1 tablespoon of beetroot juice and mix them into a paste.
  3. Now take a betel leaf and apply the paste on it.
  4. Now place the betel leaf on your forehead at the location of your pain.

 Migraine

  Ingridents:

  • Hibiscus Flower
  • Pepper Seeds
  • Lemon
  • Mountain Honey

  Procedure:

  1. Take 1 hibiscus flower.
  2. Crush about 7 pepper seeds.
  3. Now add the hibiscus flower to the crushed pepper seeds and mash them.
  4. Now add the mashed paste to 100 ml of water and mix them well.
  5. After that, squeeze 1/2 lemon into the water mixture.
  6. Atlast add honey according to your sweet preference.

 Sinusitis

  Ingridents:

  • Leucas aspera
  • Raw Rice
  • Red Chilli
  • Gingelly Oil

  Procedure:

  1. First, add 100ml of gingelly oil in a pan and heat it.
  2. Now add 10gm of raw rice and 1 red chilli.
  3. Roast it for 1 minute and add the Leucas aspera flowers into it and mix it under medium heat.
  4. Once roasted, filter the oil and store it in bottle
  5. Apply it on the head once or twice a week and have a bath under hot water.
  6. Repeat the process till its cured.
