Body Pain

 Arthritis

  Ingridents:

  • Hibiscus
  • Balloon vine
  • Tailed Pepper
  • Thippili
  • Palm Jaggery

  Procedure:

  1. Take Ballon vine leaves smash it and keep it aside.
  2. Take 20ml of water and add add dried hibiscus flower and soak it for 10 min and keep the extract.
  3. Add thippili,palm jaggery and pepper and smash it until it becomes powder.
  4. Now add 20ml of hibiscus extract amd Ballon wine leaves extract with the smashed powder.
  5. Mix well and drink it.

 Knee pain

  Ingridents:

  • Gingerly oil
  • Garlic
  • Cloves
  • Chittarata
  • Neem bar

  Procedure:

  1. Add 3 pieces of Garlic,10gm Cloves,1gm Chittarata and 1gm Neem bar and smash it.
  2. Now boil the gingerly oil and add the above mixture and boil for medium heat.
  3. Now filter and apply the oil gently on the Knee.

 Back pain

  Ingridents:

  • Jeera
  • Palm Jaggery
  • Gingerly oil
  • Thippili
  • Pirandai
  • Garlic

  Procedure:

  1. Add 1 spoon of Jeera and Thippili in a bowl and smash it.
  2. Add Garlic and Pirandai grind it and keep it as paste.
  3. Without starting the fire, Add smashed powder and paste in a basin.
  4. Now start the fire and boil for 5 mins and add Palm jaggery.
  5. Now the medicine is ready,it must be consumed next day after preparation.
